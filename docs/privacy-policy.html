PokeApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

English

PokeApp does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data.

The app is designed as a Pokedex experience that lets users explore Pokemon information, search Pokemon, filter by type or generation, view details, listen to Pokemon cries, and save favorite Pokemon locally on the device.

Data Collection

PokeApp does not collect personal information from users.

Favorites

Favorite Pokemon are stored locally on the user's device. This information is not sent to the developer or to any external server.

Internet Access

PokeApp uses internet access to load public Pokemon information, images, and sounds from external public resources. The app does not use this access to collect personal data.

Third-Party Services

PokeApp may request public Pokemon data and media from third-party public sources. These services may process basic technical request information according to their own policies.

Children's Privacy

PokeApp does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: e.portillo0707@gmail.com

Spanish / Espanol

PokeApp no recopila, almacena, comparte ni vende datos personales.

La app esta disenada como una experiencia de Pokedex que permite explorar informacion de Pokemon, buscar Pokemon, filtrar por tipo o generacion, ver detalles, escuchar sonidos de Pokemon y guardar favoritos localmente en el dispositivo.

Recopilacion de datos

PokeApp no recopila informacion personal de los usuarios.

Favoritos

Los Pokemon favoritos se guardan localmente en el dispositivo del usuario. Esta informacion no se envia al desarrollador ni a servidores externos.

Acceso a internet

PokeApp usa acceso a internet para cargar informacion publica, imagenes y sonidos de Pokemon desde recursos publicos externos. La app no usa este acceso para recopilar datos personales.

Servicios de terceros

PokeApp puede solicitar datos y archivos multimedia publicos de Pokemon desde fuentes publicas de terceros. Estos servicios pueden procesar informacion tecnica basica de la solicitud segun sus propias politicas.

Privacidad de menores

PokeApp no recopila intencionalmente datos personales de menores ni de otros usuarios.
